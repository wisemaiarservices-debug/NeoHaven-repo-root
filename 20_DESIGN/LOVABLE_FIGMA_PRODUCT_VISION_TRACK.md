## Product Vision Direction

NOVA OS should look and feel like a premium enterprise command center, not a startup landing page.

Design qualities:

- Dark graphite interface
- Dense operational data
- Clear modular navigation
- Premium SaaS visual quality
- Scientific and infrastructure-focused
- Calm, credible, and investor-grade
- Built for operator decisions, not just monitoring

## Required Modules

- NOVA Command Center
- NOVA Core AI v0
- NOVA Agriculture / NeoAgro
- NOVA Energy / NeoGrid
- NOVA Infrastructure / NeoCell
- Digital Twin
- Knowledge Graph
- Recommendations
- Impact Reports

## Product Message

NOVA OS is not just a dashboard.

It is a decision intelligence layer that turns fragmented infrastructure data into operator-approved actions with measurable water, carbon, resilience, and yield impact.

## How to Route Work

1. Lovable explores high-speed product vision.
2. Figma/FigJam captures architecture and visual direction.
3. Claude prepares implementation-ready files or patch instructions.
4. Codex ports selected UI patterns into GitHub.
5. ChatGPT reviews GitHub PRs and maintains product direction.

## Do Not Do

- Do not overwrite the GitHub implementation directly from Lovable without review.
- Do not merge design-only experiments without CI/build checks.
- Do not remove deterministic fallback mode.
- Do not imply autonomous infrastructure control.
- Do not use generic startup landing-page design language.
