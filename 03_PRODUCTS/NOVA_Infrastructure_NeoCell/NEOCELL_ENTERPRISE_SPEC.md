# NeoCell Enterprise Specification

## Public Name

NOVA Infrastructure

## Internal Name

NeoCell

## Parent Platform

NOVA OS

## Mission

Enable renewable energy, agrivoltaic, municipal, remote, and climate-adaptive sites to become observable, connected, forecastable, and optimizable through NOVA OS.

## Problem

Many operators want AI-supported optimization but do not yet have the required physical and digital foundation.

Common gaps include missing sensors, poor telemetry, no edge gateway, disconnected energy and water systems, limited communication, and no Digital Twin synchronization.

## Solution

NeoCell provides a modular AI-ready deployment layer that connects physical systems to NOVA OS.

## Core Modules

- Deployments
- Gateways
- Sensor Nodes
- Energy Spine
- Water Spine
- Communications
- Edge AI Events
- Digital Twin Sync
- Maintenance
- Reports

## Product Variants

- NeoCell Agro
- NeoCell North
- NeoCell Desert
