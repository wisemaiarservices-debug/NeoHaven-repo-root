# Adaptive Energy Spine

## Purpose

The Adaptive Energy Spine is the modular physical-digital backbone behind NeoCell.

It connects:

- Energy
- Water
- Sensors
- Communications
- Edge AI
- Digital Twin
- NOVA OS

## Functions

1. Collect operational data
2. Standardize telemetry
3. Support local buffering
4. Enable edge analytics
5. Sync with NOVA OS
6. Feed Digital Twin state
7. Feed Knowledge Graph relationships
8. Support recommendations
9. Measure operational impact

## SolarHub Role

For SolarHub, NeoCell Agro can be presented as the deployment layer behind the agrivoltaic demo.
