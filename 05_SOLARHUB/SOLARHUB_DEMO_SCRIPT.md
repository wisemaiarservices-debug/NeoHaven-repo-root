# SolarHub Demo Script

## Opening

Today I am presenting NOVA OS, an AI operating system for sustainable infrastructure.

The demo shows how NOVA OS can coordinate agriculture, renewable energy, infrastructure readiness, and AI decision support for an agrivoltaic site.

## Scene 1 — Command Center

Open NOVA Command Center.

Say:

NOVA Command Center is the unified operational view. It connects NOVA Core, NeoAgro, NeoGrid, and NeoCell into one SolarHub-ready demo.

## Scene 2 — Situation

A heatwave is forecast for an agrivoltaic site.

Say:

This matters because heat increases crop stress and water demand while also affecting solar performance and battery planning.

## Scene 3 — NeoCell

Show site readiness.

Say:

NeoCell checks gateway health, sensor availability, edge battery reserve, latency, pump node health, and water node health.

## Scene 4 — NeoAgro

Show crop and water risk.

Say:

NeoAgro forecasts water demand, crop stress, soil moisture risk, and expected yield impact.

## Scene 5 — NeoGrid

Show energy optimization.

Say:

NeoGrid coordinates battery charging, solar production, grid import, and irrigation energy timing.

## Scene 6 — NOVA Core

Show recommendation and explanation.

Say:

NOVA Core explains the recommended action: shift irrigation to the evening window while reserving battery capacity and checking field readiness.

## Scene 7 — Impact

Show metrics.

Say:

The system displays expected water savings, carbon reduction, and resilience gain. This makes the recommendation operational, measurable, and explainable.

## Closing

NOVA OS is not only a dashboard. It is a decision intelligence layer for sustainable infrastructure.

With SolarHub support, this can become a validated pilot architecture for renewable and agrivoltaic sites.
