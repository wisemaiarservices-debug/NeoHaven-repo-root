# SolarHub Pitch Deck Outline

Limit: 15 slides maximum.

## Slide 1 — Cover

NOVA OS — AI Operating System for Sustainable Infrastructure

NeoHaven AI Urban Labs | SolarHub Ignition 2026

## Slide 2 — Problem

Renewable and agrivoltaic sites are data-rich but decision-poor.

Operators face fragmented dashboards, disconnected sensors, manual decisions, weak forecasting, and limited impact visibility.

## Slide 3 — Idea

NOVA OS unifies agriculture, energy, infrastructure readiness, and AI decision support into one command center.

## Slide 4 — How It Works

Observe -> Analyze -> Forecast -> Recommend -> Simulate -> Optimize -> Measure Impact

## Slide 5 — Product Architecture

- NOVA Core AI v0
- NeoAgro / NOVA Agriculture
- NeoGrid / NOVA Energy
- NeoCell / NOVA Infrastructure
- NOVA Command Center

## Slide 6 — NeoAgro

Agrivoltaic intelligence for crop health, soil moisture, water demand, yield risk, carbon, and operator recommendations.

## Slide 7 — NeoGrid

Energy intelligence for solar production, battery reserve, grid import, carbon reduction, and load timing.

## Slide 8 — NeoCell

Infrastructure readiness for gateways, sensors, edge power, latency, pump node health, and site reliability.

## Slide 9 — NOVA Core AI v0

Deterministic first AI layer for forecast, recommendations, scenario simulation, impact scoring, and assistant explanation.

## Slide 10 — Live Demo

A heatwave scenario triggers water, energy, and readiness risks. NOVA recommends a coordinated action and displays expected impact.
