## Main Pitch

NOVA OS combines Digital Twins, Knowledge Graphs, Forecast Engines, Recommendation Engines, and Optimization Engines to optimize renewable infrastructure and agrivoltaics.

## Demo Story

A heatwave is forecast for an agrivoltaic site.

- NeoAgro forecasts higher irrigation need.
- NeoGrid forecasts solar and battery impact.
- NeoCell confirms sensor and gateway readiness.
- NOVA Twin simulates baseline and optimized scenarios.
- NOVA Intelligence recommends an operator-approved action.
- The dashboard shows water, energy, carbon, and resilience impact.
